Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Oregano
  • Red chili flakes
  • Ricotta
  • Mozarella

Directions

  1. Soak lasagna noodles in room-temperature water
  2. While noodles are soaking, place a thin layer of crushed tomatoes on the bottom of your baking dish
  3. Sprinkle a layer of salt, oregano, and chili powder on the tomato layer

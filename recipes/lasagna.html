Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Canned crushed tomatoes
  • Oregano
  • Red chili flakes
  • Ricotta
  • Mozarella
  • Olive oil

Directions

  1. Soak lasagna noodles in room-temperature water
  2. While noodles are soaking, place a thin layer of crushed tomatoes on the bottom of your baking dish
  3. Sprinkle a layer of salt, oregano, and chili powder on the tomato layer
  4. Add a layer of noodles
  5. Place a thin layer of ricotta on top of the noodle layer
  6. Add another layer of noodles
  7. Continue to alternate between tomatoes, noodles, and ricotta layers until you run out of ingredients, or you fill the tin
  8. Cover with tin foil and bake at [temp] for 45 minutes, uncover, and bake for another 15 minutes
  9. Let cool before serving
